About Us


Electrical and Computer Engineering Conference (JEEC) is based on a set of activities that promote student integration in the business world, reckon already 17 editions. The activities are free and are opened to a community of more than 11.000 students.
JEEC is an event organized by students and thought exclusively for students, we rely with support of a student association (NEEC), Electrical and Computer Engineering department (DEEC), and our university IST.

Mission

Our main objective is to bring students closer to the best companies of the job market.

Mission


JEEC seek to create connections between the academic and the business world, helping the contact of IST students, especially the Electrical and Computer Engineering students, with the companies and/or the research programs that develop new things in the Engineering field. JEEC look for to publicize MEEC, standing out the respective career prospects, and the IST itself, with the intent of further the institution towards the most influential companies in Portugal.

What we do?


The JEEC provide the students a set of diverse activities that allow an interaction with the business world.
During the one-week event there is a technology exhibition, where it can be found several companies and research engineering projects that are available to discuss and enlighten the student’s questions and to present activities developed by the company itself.
Besides the technology exhibition, the Lectures and the Discussions Panels instigate the presentation of a current topic, giving always the opportunity for students to expose their questions and/or ideas.
The student’s community can also count with several workshops, and more recently in the JEEC history, Matchmaking sessions. This is an exclusive JEEC activity, where small groups of students talk directly with company’s representatives, in a casual talk which allow to introduce themselves and their resumes, besides to acknowledge the company’s work and history.
