FASTMEETING


Do you know what Fastmeeting is?

Fastmeeting is an event organized by NEECIST, the Student's Association from the Masters in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science degree. Its goal is to explain to the students which Masters' fields they might pursue in the future. This event is divided in two segments. In the first one, students will have the opportunity to listen to a detailed explanation concerning each Masters' field, which will be given by the responsible Professor of each field. The second part will be a Q&A session with students that are currently attending the different Masters' fields, which will then be followed by a coffee break, where younger students will have the opportunity to ask any other questions left in a more informal way. This activity has already been held for 4 different occasions, but this will be the first year where it will be part of JEEC.In case you want to know more informations about the event, click here.
