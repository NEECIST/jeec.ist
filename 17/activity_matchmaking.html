MATCHMAKING

Do you know what Matchmaking is?

There are 4 companies represented in each Matchmaking session, each one sitting in a different table. Students who are registered in the session are divided into groups of 5 or 6 elements and are then assigned to a table where they can talk with the company representative. After a 20 minute cycle, the groups sit in different tables so they can get to know the remaining companies.

In this activity, students will have the possibility to gain further knowledge about the participating companies and, in some cases, even find a job opportunity. Conversely, companies will have an unique opportunity to promote their brand and get to know students that will soon be looking for a job .

If you're a student, prepare your pitch beforehand, talking about yourself, your experience and what you pretend in the future. Print your CV and don't waste this opportunity JEEC is giving you! In order to participate in these sessions, you will need to register first. Registrations will take place in a date closer to the event and will be announced in due time.
