IST/CERN Collaboration

As a result of the recently established collaboration agreement between IST and CERN, students and recent graduates from Técnico have the opportunity to become CERN associate members for up to three years, where they can collaborate in projects within different research areas of information and communication technologies, including (but not limited to):

  • information systems
  • databases
  • accelerators control software
  • industrial control
  • operating systems
  • distributed systems
  • infrastructures

    • This unique and challenging opportunity allows students to complement their university studies with practical experience in real-world research projects at a world-class institution like CERN, as well as a rather attractive monthly stipend package.

    The deadline for the first call for application is on November 22nd, 2015.

    For more informations please visit the collaboration website IST/CERN.

Ericsson Innovation Awards

The Ericsson Innovation Awards 2016 is an open competition for students all around the world.Este ano o tema trata o futuro da vida urbana, em especial as cidades inteligentes.

This year the competition is all about cities. Today, more people live in cities than in rural areas, and by 2050, about 70 percent of the world’s population is expected to reside in urban areas. Ensuring that our cities are creative, connected and sustainable is a major challenge but also an opportunity to improve the lives of billions of people along with the health and future of the planet itself.

Register your team until November 30th and December 18th the 10 semifinalists are announced. If you manage to get to this stage you will have the chance to work with Ericsson specialists.

The Grand Final is scheduled to take place in Stockholm where 4 teams will battle for first place

For more informations please visit Ericsson Innovation Awards.
