Activities

A new edition, a new vision!

  • Matchmaking

    It is based on a session with the purpose of connecting companies to students â€“ a cooperative experience.

    For students, the objective is to achieve clarifications about the business world and the technical work developed by the company. For the companies, it is an opportunity of promotion and possible recruitment. This session will include groups of three to five students and one or two speakers from each company at each table. These representatives will change tables in order to give the opportunity to all the students to have knowledge on the companies and on what they are looking for.

  • Round Table

    It consists on a debate between three to five speakers on a particular technical subject. Speakers could introduce themselves describing the company to which they work for and their tasks and functions. They could express their point of view on the subject to be debated. The round table will have a moderator and interaction with the audience is expected.

  • Stand in the Technological Exposition

    The Technological Exposition consists on a set of expositions that will take place in the inner area of the IST Central Pavilion. They must fit one or several of the following aspects:

    • Former or current projects in DEEC;
    • Projects of MEEC students;
    • Stand acquired by a company or entity;
    • NEECIST stand.

    The presence of companies in the Technological Exposition should be as dynamic as possible. This may include a demonstration or exposition of Electrical and Computer Engineering technology in order to attract the attention of the viewers. It could also be used for recruiting and divulging internships, courses and other special programmes addresses to all IST students.

  • Lectures

    Lecture slots reserved to the company, making an introduction, diffusing one or more products, developing briefings on technical subjects and even recruiting staff. An approach on technical subjects would be of most convenience, being these subject connected to the work developed by the company.
