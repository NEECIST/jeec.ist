Telecommunications Panel - "A View on the 5th Generation of Mobile Communications"

  • Prof. Luis M. Correia

    Moderator

  • Eng. Luis Muchacho

    Speaker

  • Eng. José Freire

    Speaker

  • Eng. Luis Miguel Santo

    Speaker

The telecommunications panel takes place in the 10th of March, at 11h00 in Salão Nobre of the Pavilhão Central on the subject “A View on the 5th Generation of Mobile Communications”..

Moderador

Professor Luís M. Correia, Professor in DEEC since 1991, Researcher of the Telecommunications Institute since 1994, is a name of huge reputation of this Department, recognized by all the students since the 1st year until the 2nd grade, by the wide range of approach of the matters that he teaches, such as: MEEC Portfolio (which he introduced), Management of Engineering Projects, Entrepreneurship, Innovation and Transference of Technology, Systems of Mobile Communications. His large CV includes also Curricular Units of the Master in Physical and Technological Engineering or Master in Technology Management. Known for the strong link to the business world, he makes it one of the key spots of the Master Thesis that he supervises.

Oradores

  • Luis Muchacho, from Ericsson, one of the leader companies in the Telecommunications area for selling products and services
  • José Freire, from Impresa, a media group that takes part in major scenarios such as SIC, Expresso and Visão
  • Luis Miguel Santo, from NOS, one of the “queens” of Portuguese media, operating in multimedia and telecommunications area.

It is a great opportunity to hear about a subject of the future, with professionals of companies of great reputation in their area of activity. The moderator is one of the most charismatic and famous professors of DEEC.

Panel of Systems, Decision and Control - "Role of Technological PMEs in Innovator Portugal: Examples in Robotics Area"

  • Prof. Pedro Lima

    Moderator

  • Eng. André Almeida

    Speaker

  • Eng. Paulo Alvito

    Speaker

  • Eng. Filipe Jesus

    Speaker

  • Eng. Nelson Ramos

    Speaker

  • Eng. Marco Barbosa

    Speaker

The Panel of Systems, Decision and Control takes place in 8th of March, at 13h30 in Salão Nobre of the Pavilhão Central, on the subject “Role of Technological PMEs in Innovator Portugal: Examples in Robotics Area.".

Moderador

Professor Pedro Lima is present in the scholar life of all students of this area since the 2nd grade of Systems, Decision and Control as he is the Responsible for the Curricular Unit of Autonomous Systems. He is also a Researcher in Institute of Systems and Robotics and he is responsible for many projects such as the MOnarCH (robots for interaction with children in the IPO) or INSIDE (robots for interaction with children with development disabilities). He was one of the pioneers of SocRob (Soccer Robots or Society of Robots), which participated in several World Robotics Championships.

Oradores

  • André Almeida, CEO/CTO from Artica CC, a company that combines art and technology in their products.
  • Paulo Alvito, CEO from IDMind, a company that develops innovator products in the areas of Robotics, Automation and Mechatronics.
  • Filipe Jesus, from μRoboptics, a consultant linked to Robotics, Computer Vision and Systems Quality Control.
  • Nelson Ramos, from SMARTIF, a company that offers home automation solutions for homes, offices and hotels.
  • Marco Barbosa, CEO from TURFLYNX, an automation company that produced an electric vehicle for the collection of golf balls.

These are companies of solid reputation, a Moderator who is one of the great professors in the area of Systems, Decision and Control and subjects for debate interesting to all the fans of the area.
